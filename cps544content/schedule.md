# Schedule

This schedule provides an overview for each week that is subject to change.  Each week there are one or more reading assignments.  Ideally, students should read the material before it is presented in class however that is not a requirement.

This schedule does not include assignments since those are posted on Canvas.  Explicit dates are not given in this schedule in an attempt to keep it reusable.  We will follow the order pretty carefully.

### Overview

Overview of UNIX/Linux, GIT, GitHub, and the POSIX shell.

### Reading

1. Chapter 1 "A Quick Review of Basics" in "Shell Programming"
1. Chapter 2 "What is the Shell" in "Shell Programming"

### Lectures

1. [Course overview](./slides/overview.md)
